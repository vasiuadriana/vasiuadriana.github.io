About this Blog

The motivation behind Mooses Love Forest blog is that writing when everything around you is in a rush means stopping and observing, enjoying the world, loving the environment around you.

The more you see and enjoy, the more you have the means to create. And the more you create, the more you want to share. Writing is a beautiful way to share - it's permanent and asynchronous!
